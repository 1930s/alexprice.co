Process

  1. We’ll see if I’m a good fit

    2. I have a skillset that’s good for some products, and not so good for others. The first discussion I’ll have with you is to see whether or not we’d be able to successfully build something. Better to figure that out early on, then after a ton of time and money has been spent.

  2. Define business objectives

    3. I’m an economics major, and I’ve run a business before. I’m here to help you achieve business objectives. We’ll figure out what those objectives are, and use that to define product strategy.

  3. Iterate, iterate, iterate

    4. We’ll have a few rounds of sketch, mockup, prototype, repeat. I’ve found that the best version of a product isn’t the first one - iterating on ideas quickly will help us uncover the best way to achieve your business goals.

  4. Ship, and evaluate

    5. After we’ve defined what the product looks like, I’ll move fast to build it—then build it again. After we’ve shipped your project, I’m not quite finished yet—I’ll help you determine whether or not the product has achieved pre-set goals, and make data-driven tweaks to get as close to success as possible.
